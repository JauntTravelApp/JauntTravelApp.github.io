Ray Grahams - Product Page

I worked as the IT manager during the total redesign of the Ray Grahams website. This was a joint project and development was not carried out inhouse. The development studio sent us the designs for the the website, most of which were great however the product page just didn't have a nice feel.

I spent sometime working on the design for the product page making sure all the important aspects such as image, price, add to basket button and availability were all above the fold. This experience taugh me that sitting down with the client and talking to them about their needs is a very important stage of the design process.
