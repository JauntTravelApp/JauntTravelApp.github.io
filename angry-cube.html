Angry Cube

Angry Cube had quite an old looking logo but a cool brand so when asked I knew that I'd definitely be able to help them. The done thing this year is a simple text logo having seen most of the largest companies going down this route. But for this brand a san serif font wasn't going to cut it!

Although basic the aggressive font style sets this logo apart for all the rest, giving a lasting impression, not to mention it looks great on thier products!
