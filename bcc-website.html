Belfast Coffee Co - Website

The Belfast Coffee Co needed a website that was going to allow them to sell their beans all over the globe. I went for a very simple design that didnt take away from the great product they were selling. This allowed users to focus and find the right beans and making a purchase.

During the first few months I use Hotjar with the site to see how people were interacting with the site. This gave me the informtion needed to shuffle bits on the the home and product pages to improve the conversion rate by over 7% on desktop and 5% mobile just in time for their first Christmas ramp up.
