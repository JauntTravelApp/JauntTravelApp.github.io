Belfast Coffee Co - Bag

The Belfast Coffee Co were the newest coffee roasters in town but they needed a product that was going to jump out at people on the already crowded shelves. That's where I came in.

Their bags were going to be the first impression that anyone was going to have of the company therefore attention for detail was very important. The finished product was a recycled brown paper bag with a printed recycled cardboard wrap with a field of solid colour that was different for each type of beans the company sold. The text was rustic yet bold and delivered the companies message at first glance.
