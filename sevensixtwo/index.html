Sevensixtwo Watch Company
We Build Affordable Luxury Watches

A simple, yet elegant watch collection from Northern Ireland. Assembled by hand and individually customised to suit your style.

Our design process started in summer 2016 with countless hours of research into vintage style watches and modern day minimalist watches. We made and binned prototype after prototype until we had developed a watch that ticked all the right boxes for us.

This finalised with us creating what you see today, a big crown, manual winding, pilot style watch and a minimalist quartz watch sporting the same dial design.

Why should you back Sevensixtwo?

It's simple really. Yes, we are asking you to fund our company and yes, in return you will receive the watch that you have chosen. The big difference here is; you are not just buying a watch, you are kick starting our business.
