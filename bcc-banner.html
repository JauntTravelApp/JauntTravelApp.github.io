Belfast Coffee Co - Banners

The Belfast Coffee Co came back to and wanted banners for each of the coffee origins they sold to make it easy for people visualise. Again this had to make a good first and lasting impression and even drive a sale on their website.

Each banner really captures a sense of the region that each of the beans are grown in. The background image shows the terrain in which the beans are grown with the images ontop really setting the picture off. The banners also display select colours and characteristics from the country.
