Jaunt - App

Visit online

Jaunt is a social travel app that encourages uses to collaborate at every stage of their travels. Jaunt is my very own project that has allowed me to really master my skills of design, development and leadership. Design has been one of the most important aspects of this project as there are other travel sites out there but none are very easy on the eye or to use. I know this has been a pain point for most in the past but I was going to change this.

Over a year and thousands of responses from the desk and field research the following designs have been produced. This will not be the final designs either as the newest revisions need to under focus group for feedback. The app was the first project I have undertaken that involved no consideration to desktop design principles allowing me to truely show what can be done with mobile first thinking.

Role
UX / UI, Research, Development, Management
