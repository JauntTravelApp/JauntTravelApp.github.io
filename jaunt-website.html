Jaunt - Website

Visit online

Jaunt is a social travel app that encourages uses to collaborate at every stage of their travels. The Jaunt web app came after the designs and development had been completed for the android app. This gave me a real challenge, how do I give the feel of the app on a large desktop? Good thing I love a challenge then. I studied the app and finally set about drawing up a few ideas on on Adobe XD. I have to admit I was quite impressed and very happy with what I had designed.

The web app is completely responsive and has replaced the app as the alpha testing platform for Jaunt. This means all future features will come to web first and then to the apps. Now the challenge s reversed. I have to continuely think about the future design, making sure that what I'm making for the responsive web would look and feel natural on a native app.
