Bank of Invention - Website

Visit online

As one of the 4 volunteer organisers of the Bank of Invention I decided that I would design and make a website for them. It was a great chance for me to go wild with the design and I'd always wanted to make a site using material design principles.

After completing the designs that conformed to the material design principles I started to develop the website. I actually really enjoyed working with material design and would like to give it another go for a client.
